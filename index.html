Our Title Services

We provide comprehensive title search and examination services tailored for title insurance companies

Title Search & Examination

Comprehensive property title searches including ownership history, liens, encumbrances, and legal descriptions. Our experienced team ensures thorough examination of all relevant records.

Title Commitments

Detailed title commitment preparation with accurate schedules, exceptions, and requirements. We provide clear, comprehensive reports that meet industry standards.

Lien & Judgment Searches

Thorough searches for liens, judgments, bankruptcies, and other encumbrances that may affect property ownership or transfer.

Property Research

Detailed property research including deed history, tax records, survey information, and boundary descriptions for accurate title determination.

Document Preparation

Professional preparation of legal documents, contracts, and closing papers. We ensure all documentation meets regulatory standards and requirements for smooth transactions.

Property Preservation

Comprehensive property preservation services to maintain and protect real estate assets. We ensure properties are secured, maintained, and compliant with local regulations.
